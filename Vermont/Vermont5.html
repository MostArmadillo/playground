Amazing Views and Forests

If you go into Vermont's forests, you will see the nice environment of Vermont! In the Fall ,some tress will have yellow, orange, or red leaves! The trees can be very tall and the canopy of the tress can make the sky look nice. When you hiking in one of Vermont's forest, green or Fall colors are everywhere!

When you climb a mountain, you might see fascinating views! The views and show mountains, forests, rivers, lakes! You should take pictures of the amazing views of Vermont!
