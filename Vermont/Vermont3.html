National Parks

A picture of the hat Abraham Lincoin was wearing is in this image

Vermont has good national parks. The Abraham Lincoin Family house is in Vermont. There's even a park featuring a farm from 1890! Since Vermont is a small state, you can go to the parks in neighboring states. More about neighboring states of Vermont in Page 6.

You might be interested in the Abraham Family Home. The hat that Abraham Lincoin wore is on display there it is one of the three locations that displays that hat in the entire country!
