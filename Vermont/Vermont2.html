Nice and Powerful Seasons

One of the things you might notice in Vermont is the climate. If you come in the summer, it will be very hot. If you come in the Winter, it will be very colf and high chances of rainy days and sometimes snow.

If you were to come to Vermont, it is advisable to come in Spring or Early Fall because it won't be too hot or too cold.

The least advisable time to go is winter. Although it will be nice to play with rain and snow, you have to wear thermos and many layers of jackets to just take a step outside! When you come back in, you have to take off all of your jackets and thermos off.
