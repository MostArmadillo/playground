Debt

What is Debt?

Debt is money that is owed to someone because you borrowed money from them, like a credit card is an example of going into debt to someone. So if you borrow $100 from someone you would become debt with the person that gave you that money. Another way to have debt as an example is someone giving $100 in credit to someone but the person they gave the credit to would be in debt to them.

Credit Cards and Debt

A credit card is the most common way people go into debt. When you pay with a credit card, the credit card company pays for your purchase then you have to pay then back because you owe debt to them. Also if you use a credit card, credit card providers would charge interest, a fee to borrowing money, the amount of interest that is charged is a percentage of the money you borrowed. The interest comes as a bill every month so the faster you pay your debt then the interest will not be as high.

The Risks of Debt

When you go into debt, it is a risk because if you’re late on a payment, you get charged penalty fees which makes debt worse. And if you don’t pay the penalty fees or you’re late on a payment, it gets tracked on your credit history which is the permanent record of your times when you pay your debt on time so you’re responsible for your debt. or you’re not responsible when you go into debt. When you apply for a credit card, the credit provider would check your credit history, it’s like checking grades for college or a job. If you go default, stop paying your loans, the person who gave you the credit will lose money because of you and they can take you a special court where you have to give up part of your paycheck or sell some property.

Neccesary and Common times to go ito debt

This doesn’t mean you should never go into debt, it’s necessary sometimes to buy college, house, or a car. Like if you want to be a doctor but you don’t have enough money for college or medical school so you get a student loan to apply for the college or school then you repay them. So debt is pretty much an investment for the future. When people go to college, that’s when people get their first credit card and go into debt for a student loan. They normally charge 0% interest because they want new customers, then after months they charge interest to gain money after they got many customers.

Bad People with Debt

The bad thing is some people forget all about the debt and use the credit card to buy all sorts of expensive stuff thinking it’s free. It can take years just to climb out of debt. To get out of debt, you need discipline and decision making. You might have to skip things you might enjoy so you owe less money. You want to avoid the situation of having mountains of money in debt.

Businesses and Debt

Businesses always get into debt all the time. They get a bond which gives the money they need for a start in the business, they normally get these bonds from the government. The government uses the money you gave them (taxes), to pay for construction workers, military, etc aswell as giving out buisness loans. Then when the business is successful, the business repays the government to get out of debt so they don’t get penalty fees.
