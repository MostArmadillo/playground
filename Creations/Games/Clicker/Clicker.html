Clicker

Instructions

There are 64 boxes, 1 box will have the name Click Me!, which is what you want to click, while the others are spelled incorrectly like "Cilk Me!." The box that has the "Click Me!" will change every 20 seconds. You will get an alert if you click on a mispelled box but clicking on the correct box will transport you to the next level. Also the timer runs out every 6 slides then brings you back to the previous level (this is the first level so it will refresh instead)
